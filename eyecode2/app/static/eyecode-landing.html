The Experiment

This study is being conducted by the Percepts and Concepts Laboratory in the Cognitive Science Program at Indiana University. We conduct experiments on how people acquire conceptual knowledge and how conceptual knowledge impacts perception.

If you agree to participate, you will be asked to predict the output of 10 simple Python programs. There is a brief survey before the experiment with questions about your gender, age, education level, and programming experience.

Requirements

You must be able to read and understand simple Python programs (ifs, loops, functions, basic classes). You may also only complete this experiment once.

What to do
